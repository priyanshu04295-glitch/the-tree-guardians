Trees We Have Planted

Every tree species plays a unique role in maintaining ecological balance. We focus on planting trees that improve biodiversity, strengthen local ecosystems and provide long-term environmental benefits.


🌿

Neem Tree

Scientific Name: Azadirachta indica

• Excellent natural air purifier.

• Well known for its medicinal properties.

• Highly drought-resistant and easy to maintain.

• Supports healthier surroundings and biodiversity.

🌳

Peepal Tree

Scientific Name: Ficus religiosa

• Supports numerous bird and insect species.

• Helps improve ecological balance.

• Provides extensive shade.

• One of the most valuable native trees.

🥭

Mango Tree

Scientific Name: Mangifera indica

• Produces nutritious fruits.

• Creates natural shade.

• Supports wildlife and pollinators.

• Contributes to greener communities.

🌲

Ashoka Tree

Scientific Name: Saraca asoca

• Attractive evergreen tree.

• Helps reduce urban pollution.

• Commonly planted along roads and parks.

• Enhances landscape beauty.
